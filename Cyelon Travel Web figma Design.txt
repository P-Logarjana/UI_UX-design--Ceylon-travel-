

------Overview-------
Ceylon Travel is a modern and visually appealing UI/UX design 
concept created in Figma, aimed at enhancing the user 
experience for exploring Sri Lanka's tourism destinations. 
The design features a clean and intuitive interface, ensuring seamless 
navigation for users looking for travel destinations, accommodations, 
and activities across Sri Lanka.
